## 🚀 3521. Find Product Recommendation Pairs

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to identify product pairs that are frequently purchased together by the same customers.

We use a **self join** on the `ProductPurchases` table to generate product pairs for each user. Then, we join the `ProductInfo` table to retrieve the category of each product. Finally, we count how many distinct customers purchased each pair and keep only those purchased together by at least **3 customers**.

---

## 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT pp1.product_id AS product1_id, pp2.product_id AS product2_id,
pi1.category AS product1_category, pi2.category AS product2_category,
COUNT(DISTINCT pp1.user_id) AS customer_count
FROM ProductPurchases pp1
INNER JOIN ProductPurchases pp2 ON pp1.user_id = pp2.user_id AND pp1.product_id < pp2.product_id
INNER JOIN ProductInfo pi1 ON pi1.product_id = pp1.product_id
INNER JOIN ProductInfo pi2 ON pi2.product_id = pp2.product_id
GROUP BY product1_id, product2_id
HAVING customer_count >= 3
ORDER BY customer_count DESC, product1_id, product2_id;
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Self join the `ProductPurchases` table on `user_id` to pair products purchased by the same customer.
2. Use `pp1.product_id < pp2.product_id` to generate each product pair only once and avoid duplicate or reversed pairs.
3. Join the `ProductInfo` table twice to retrieve the category of both products.
4. Group by each product pair and count the **distinct customers** who purchased both products.
5. Use `HAVING` to keep only pairs purchased together by at least **3 customers**, then sort the result.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n²)`

**Space Complexity:** `O(n)`
