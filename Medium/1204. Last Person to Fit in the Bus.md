## 🚀 1204. Last Person to Fit in the Bus

### 💡 Idea

The goal is to find the **last person who can board the bus** without exceeding the weight limit of **1000 kg**.

We calculate the **running total** (cumulative sum) of the passengers' weights in the order of their turn. The last person whose cumulative weight is less than or equal to **1000** is the answer.

---

## 💻 Solution

```sql
SELECT person_name
FROM (
    SELECT person_name, turn,
    SUM(weight) OVER (ORDER BY turn) AS total_weight
    FROM Queue
) t
WHERE total_weight <= 1000
ORDER BY total_weight DESC
LIMIT 1;
```

---

### 🛠️ How It Works

1. Sort the passengers by their `turn`.
2. Use a window function to calculate the cumulative (`running`) sum of weights.
3. Keep only the passengers whose cumulative weight is **≤ 1000**.
4. Among them, the passenger with the largest cumulative weight is the last one who can board.
5. Return that passenger's name.

---

### ⏱️ Complexity

**Time Complexity:** `O(n log n)` *(sorting by `turn` for the window function)*

**Space Complexity:** `O(n)` *(stores the running total for each passenger)*
